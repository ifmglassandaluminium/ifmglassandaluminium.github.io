About Us

business

Who we are?

IFM Glass and Aluminium is a private company that installs aluminium doors and windows for houses, offices, restuarants and more. Our services are there to ensure that there is improvement at your home, workplace or any other places that need our touch.

build

What we do?

Our business is simple. We do any aluminum work, replacement, repairs that suits your vision and style. We target people that are constructing new buildings or renovations such as Houses, Office Spaces, Shops, Restuarants, etc... To install for them Aluminium glass doors and windows. We also handle repair/replace damaged or broken windows for people that already have these utilities.

face

Why choose us?

The best part of our business is that our services are extremely affordable and yet still highly competitive. We ensure that our clients are satisfied at all times and that has been our trademark thus far. Elegance and style is what we do best.
