About Us

business

Who we are?

IFM Glass and Aluminium is a private company that installs aluminium doors and windows for houses, offices, restuarants and more. Our services are there to ensure that there is improvement at your home, workplace or any other places that need our touch. We are highly dedicated to transforming buildings using glass and aluminium which is both strong and beautiful. Our team of hardworking and dedicated individuals can turn an ordinary place to look stylish and modern.

What we do?

Our business is simple. We do any aluminum work, replacement, repairs that suits your vision and style. We target people that are constructing new buildings or renovations such as Houses, Office Spaces, Shops, Restuarants, etc... To install for them Aluminium glass doors and windows. We also repair/replace damaged or broken windows for people that already have these utilities. You simply contact us and we get your information and measurements to where you would like your windows or doors to be installed and then we do our magic. It is as simple as that. Customer satisfaction is our middle name.
